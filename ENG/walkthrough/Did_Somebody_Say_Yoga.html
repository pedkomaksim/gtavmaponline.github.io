Did Somebody Say Yoga

100% Gold Requirements for Did Somebody Say Yoga?
Mission Time Complete within 15:00
Warrior Complete the first yoga pose without fault
Triangle Complete the second pose without fault
Praise the Sun! Complete the third pose without fault

PART 1: YOGA FLAMES

When the mission begins, follow the onscreen prompts to learn several moves for doing Yoga.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "WARRIOR"

Follow the prompts to complete the first pose.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "TRIANGLE"

Follow the prompts to complete the second pose.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "PRAISE THE SUN!"

Follow the prompts to complete the third pose.

PART 2: FATHER & SON TIME

 

After the third pose, a cutscene occurs. From there, go to Jimmy's room and summon him to the car.

 

Drive over to the waypoint at the Burger Shot with Jimmy and then drive back to the house to trigger a chain of events.

PART 3: THE DRUG TRIP

 

Various trippy cutscenes occur followed by Michael flying though the air in a drugged-out state. To complete the "flying" segment, move the Left Analog Stick upwards to make Michael crash to the ground.

 

After Michael wakes up from his stupor, steal the bicycle nearby and go back to the house to complete the mission.
