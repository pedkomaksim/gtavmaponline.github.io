Nervous Ron

100% Gold Requirements for Nervous Ron
Headshots Kill 5 enemies with headshots
Mission Time Complete within 12:30
Nervous Twitch Win the race against Ron
6 Bridges, 1 Plane Fly under 6 bridges along the flight path
Death on a Wing Kill all bikers while laying on the plane wing

PART 1: THE STAKEOUT

 

Drop by Ammunation and grab a Sniper Rifle with an Advanced Scope and Suppressor.

 

With the rifle in hand, meet up with Ron over by the water tower and climb it to the top to start sniping some bad guys.

PART 2: MIDNIGHT SNIPING

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "HEADSHOTS"

Cover Ron’s approach, picking off guards as he spies out the gang’s position. Be sure to aim at their heads for headshots!

 

Several of the guards will appear near or above the radio tower area. Also, take out the two lights here when Ron prompts it. As Ron places the bomb onto the tank, a biker approaches, so quickly shoot him.

 

Once Ron places the bomb, a rival helicopter swoops in for some fun. Aim at the pilot and snipe him to bring down the chopper.

PART 3: RAIDING THE AIRFIELD

 

From there, jump down and run towards the air hangar while shooting down Lost MC members. Get into cover and take them out one by one and get into the hangar and jump on the plane.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "DEATH ON A WING"

When Trevor gets onto the wing, fire at all the Lost members that show up on the GPS (as red dots). There will be TWO vans and a biker behind the wing, so start firing!

PART 4: STEALING THE GOODS

 

Once all the Lost members are down, get onto your plane and fly outta there! Put up the landing gears and then use a knife move to get the Lost member off your wing.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "6 BRIDGES, 1 PLANE"

As you follow Ron through the hills, be sure to fly UNDER the bridges that are in your flight path. There are a total of six bridges to and from the airfield so be sure to get them all!

 

Once you get to the drop-off point, drop the package into the yellow smoke and then fly back to the airfield in one piece to complete the mission.
