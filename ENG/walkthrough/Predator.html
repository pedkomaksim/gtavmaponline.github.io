Predator

MISSION NOTE

If you start the mission as Trevor or Michael, you'll be treated to an opening cutscene but if you switch to Franklin after he sends his text message to Trevor, the opening cutscene is omitted and the mission automatically begins.
100% Gold Requirements for Predator
Accuracy Finish with a shooting accuracy of at least 70%
Time Complete within 09:00
Headshots Kill 3 enemies with a headshots
Thin the Herd Don't kill any animals

PART 1: THE CHASE

 

Get into Trevor's car and drive towards Franklin's location and the game switches to Franklin on hot pursuit of the O'Neil brothers.

 

Watch for the group to break down the dirt row to the left side of the highway. After a few sharp turns, the target vehicle will veer to miss a deer and roll off the road. Exit your vehicle and run down towards the burning car to trigger a phone call to Trevor.

PART 2: THE PREDATOR

 

After the switch, drive towards the airfield and get in the Frogger and fly out to Raton Canyon. Fly towards the waypoint to start the sniping segment as Michael.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "THIN THE HERD/ ACCURACY/ HEADSHOTS"

Avoid shooting any animals (and watch out for the Sasquatch!). The elk and deer should be easy to distinguish, so don't get too trigger happy. Also, when sniping, don't hesitate to use MIke's special ability for better aim and accuracy.

 

The first target is in the river taking a leisurely swim. Use Mike's special ability to get a good headshot on him.

 

The second target is hiding behind a tree in the middle of the forest. You'll have to wait for him to move out of cover and away from the trees to ID him (otherwise, you'll waste bullets as he hides). Again, use Mike's special ability to get a good headshot. For the third target, the game switches to Franklin.

PART 3: THE LAST ONE

 

Follow Chop as he runs through the river and up the hill. The third target is running away up the dirt road, so let Chop get him. From here, you can shoot him down as Franklin or switch to Michael and go for a headshot (be careful not to shoot Chop or Franklin!).

 

Once the third target is down, switch to Trevor and land the chopper on the dirt road to pick up Franklin and Chop. Fly back to the airfield and land the chopper to complete the mission.
