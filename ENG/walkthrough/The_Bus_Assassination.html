MISSION NOTE: INVESTMENT OPPORTUNITY

Follow Lester's advice and do NOT invest in any shares for the Vapid (VAP) stock until you've completed the mission

 

When the mission begins, get in your vehicle and drive over to the bus lot. Take the indicated bus and start picking up passengers en route to the target.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "hit and run bonus"

For the bonus, avoid using any weapons on the target and hit him with the bus. If you're lucky, the target will get smashed by another car, giving you the ripe opportunity to squash him! Once that's done, quickly escape any unwanted heat to complete the mission.

 

After the mission, switch to all three characters and invest all your money in Vapid (VAP) stocks. Give your investment about 2-3 in-game days to reach 100% and then SELL your shares for maximum profit!
