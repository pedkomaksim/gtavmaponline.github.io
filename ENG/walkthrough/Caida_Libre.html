Caida Libre

100% Gold Requirements for Caida Libre
Floor It Reach top speed on the Sanchez
Glued to the Seat Don't fall off the Sanchez during the race
One Two Three Shoot down the plane with 3 shots
Time Complete within 09:45

MISSION NOTE: DIFFERENT STARTS

If you begin the mission as Michael, a short cutscene will show Mike and Trevor arriving to the house at the same time. But if you decide to start the mission with Trevor, Mike will already be at the house and waiting at the front door (see screencap above).

PART 1: THE SECRET WEAPON

 

When the mission begins, get into your vehicle and drive up to the waypoint at the observatory. The van  with the special weapon is located just near the grassy area.

 

Get into the van to start the sniping sequence. The airplane will fly by on the horizon, so zoom in as close as possible to see the RED target reticle.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "ONE TWO THREE"

The trick to shooting down the plane in just three shots is to NOT aim directly at the RED reticle. As the RED reticle moves, move the gun away from its path and then shoot a bullet just as the reticle is about to get within the gun's target range. If done correctly, the bullet will hit in one shot.


PART 2: THE MOTORCYCLE CHASE

 

Carefully aim the gun at the RED reticle to shoot down the airplane engine. Shoot the reticle three separate times to bring the plane down and to switch to Trevor.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "FLOOR IT/ GLUED TO THE SEAT"

To achieve these requirements, Trevor needs to go at FULL SPEED on the Sanchez during the ENTIRE chase. From the top of the mountain all the way to the crash site, you must NOT let go of the gas pedal or slow down at any time! On top of that, Trevor must not fall off the bike, so carefully maneuver through the course.

As Trevor descends the mountaintop, keep the pedal to the metal and zoom down. Keep the plane in your trajectory and follow it in the direction it flies towards.

 

There will be several obstacles such as stunt jumps, so watch out for those! Missing a jump will result in losing some Gold requirements! Weave your way thru the desert and thru traffic carefully but remember not to let go of the gas pedal at any time!

PART 3: TAKE THE PACKAGE

 

When you arrive at the crash site, shoot down Madrazo's cousin and enter the airplane to retrieve the briefcase and then switch to Michael.

PART 4: DESTROY THE SECRET WEAPON

 

Drive the van to a secure location and then destroy it with either a Sticky Bomb or the Jerry can.

MISSION NOTE: LOWER MISSION TIME

One option to take for a lower mission time is to drive the gun van directly to the steel mill (where the ending cutscene takes place) and blow up the van there. This will save several minutes from trying to find another vehicle had you destroyed it elsewhere.


 

Once that's done, drive to the waypoint to meet with Trevor and to complete the mission.
