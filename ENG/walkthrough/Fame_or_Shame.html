Fame or Shame

100% Gold Requirements for Fame Or Shame
Fastest Speed Reach top speed in the phantom
Bearing Down Stay close to Lazlow throughout the chase
No, I Can Park Here Knock out the event coordinator
All Hooked Up Don't unhook the trailer

PART 1: CATCHING UP WITH FRIENDS

 

After the cutscene, jump into Michael's car and drive over to the Maze Bank Arena. Park the car at the waypoint and make your way inside the arena.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENT: "NO, I CAN PARK HERE"

To achieve this requirement, walk up to the gentleman with the clipboard that says, "You can't park there!" and smack him with a punch to the head.

 

After the cutscenes that ensue, run outside of the arena and take the big truck and chase down Lazlow with it (if you want to unhook the trailer, do it before chasing Lazlow for faster speed).

PART 2: CHASING DOWN LAZLOW

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENT: "ALL HOOKED UP"

To achieve this requirement, drive fast but carefully so that Trevor doesn't accidentally unhook the trailer. The oncoming traffic can easily smash the trailer off, so watch your speed!

 

When following Lazlow up the streets, he will veer onto the railroads for a few seconds. It is NOT necessary to follow him on the tracks, so just stay on the road and keep an eye on his route.

 

The point of the chase is NOT to harm or kill Lazlow, so avoid using any weapons or trying to ram him off the road! Follow him all the way into the LS River to complete the mission.
