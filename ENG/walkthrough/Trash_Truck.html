Trash Truck

100% Gold Requirements for Trash Truck
Mission Time Complete within 5:00
Undented Deliver the Trashmaster with no damage
In the Dust Reach top speed in the Trashmaster
 

Drive over to the Trash truck location and then walk up to it and steal it from the driver. Doing this will net you a 2-Star Wanted level, so immediately exit the Trash truck (leaving it behind won't fail the mission) and look for some safe cover from the cops. After evading the cops, take the Trash truck and drive it over to the FIB lot.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "UNDENTED/ IN THE DUST"

For the "Undented" requirement, you'll need to be super careful driving the truck to the FIB lot with no dings, dents, or scratches.

For the "In The Dust" requirement, drive the trash truck at blazing speed for about 10-15 seconds to satisfy the requirement. The trash truck is a pretty slow vehicle so look for a downhill slope to get some speed.
