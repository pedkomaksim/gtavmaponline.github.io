Surveying the Score

 Follow the vans without being warned on distance
Cavity Search
 Find the construction hole within 0:20 seconds

 Fly under the bridge while following vans
Tunnel Flight
 Fly through the tunnel while following vans
Mission Time
 Complete within 11:00

MISSION NOTE: WHICH CHARACTER?

There are three different intro scenes to this mission depending on which character you choose.

  • For the full intro scene, start the mission as Trevor.
  • For a mid-scene intro, start the mission as Michael.
  • And for a shortened intro, start the mission as Franklin.

PART 1: CASING THE JOINT

 

After the opening cutscene, get into Mike's car and travel to the Depository entrance. From there, drive to the underpass and park the car across the street to trigger the next objective.

PART 2: EYE INTHE SKY

 

Drive to the Sandy Shores airfield and get in the Frogger and fly out to the city to ID the security vans.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "PERFECT DISTANCE"

For the "Perfect Distance" requirement, stay within a half block from the security vans to avoid detection.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "UNDER THE BRIDGE"

For the "Under The Bridge" requirement, fly under the nearest bridge as the vans drive past the LS River. You can't miss it!

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "TUNNEL FLIGHT"

For the "Tunnel Flight" requirement, the vans will enter the narrow tunnel and you'll have to follow them all the way thru. You MUST make it out at the other end of the tunnel for the requirement to count!

 

Once the security vans reach their destination, a quick cutscene will show the vans arriving at the Depository. From here, Lester requests to find the construction site nearby. Turn LEFT to the red and white building for the next objective.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "CAVITY SEARCH"

The construction site with the "big ass hole" is to the left of the Arcadia Center. Veer left of the building and you should be able to see it within a few seconds.

 

Hover very low over the construction site until Lester gives the okay to leave.

 

From here, you can either fly back to the airfield with Trevor or switch to Michael and drop off Franklin at his house in Vinewood Hills to complete the mission. Your choice!
