Daddy's Little Girl

100% Gold Requirements for Daddy's Little Girl
Fastest Speed Reach top speed on the Seashark
Stabilizer Don't fall off the bicycle
Faster Than Fish Swim to the boat within 1 minute


PART 1: BICYCLE RACE

 

When the mission begins, get into the vehicle and drive to the waypoint at Vespucci Beach. Walk up to the nearby bicycle rental stand and choose any of the bikes to begin the race.

 

Bicycling is fairly straightforward. Tap the run button to pedal more rapidly, but watch out for Michael’s health. He’s not in great shape, and if you push too hard he’ll begin to lose health. That being said, Jimmy is much slower, so just pedal towards the pier from any direction.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "STABILIZER"

To avoid falling off the bike, just pedal in the direction that features less pedestrians. The left side of the beach (the boardwalk) has less obstacles but is twisty while the main walkway is straight but has more people in the way. Slow down to avoid collisions!

After the race, Jimmy reveals the real reason for going to the beach. From there, jump into the water and swim towards the Dignity and climb aboard to trigger a cutscene.

PART 2: THE RESCUE

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "FASTER THAN FISH"

In order for Mike to swim to the boat faster, he'll need Max Stamina (which he won't have on a first playthrough), so Replay the mission when Mike is maxed to achieve the Gold

 

Once Tracey is aboard the jetski, gun it towards the tunnel just down the beach from the ship.

PART 3: THE ESCAPE

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "FASTEST SPEED"

The jetski is fairly easy to control even at high speed, so slam on the gas and maneuver at a high speed as much as possible without letting go of the gas pedal (even when making turns) to get the Gold.


  

Follow the waterways going to the east side of the canals until you reach the marina area. Make a left turn at the marina and then go to the right towards the ocean. As you approach the ocean, make a U-turn at the small peninsula (see screencap above) and Michael will evade the baddies.

 

Once the baddies are gone, jet back to the beach and meet up with Jimmy to complete the mission.

POST-MISSION CUTSCENE

 

After the mission, Mike takes a quiet smoke break at the Pier. Note that this cutscene is NOT available on a Replay.
