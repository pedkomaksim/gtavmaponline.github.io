I Fought The Law

100% Gold Requirements for I Fought The Law...
Tight Squeeze Race between the two trucks
Bus Passed Race between the two busses
Follow the Leader
 Follow Trevor through the tunnel
Mission Time Complete within 12 minutes
Split Seconds Use Franklin's special ability during the race

PART 1: COUNTRYSIDE DRIVE

 

After the cutscene, enter the car and drive to the waypoint up near the countryside to trigger a cutscene.

PART 2: THE RACE

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "TIGHT SQUEEZE/ BUS PASSED/ SPLIT SECONDS"

During the race, Franklin will pass by a duo of trucks and a duo of busses. Use his special ability to squeeze between them both and grab all three requirements easily.

PART 3: POLICE CHASE

 

The point of the race is NOT to win against the racers but to lead them to a trap. Continue racing until they reach the bridge area, in which Michael and Trevor take over.

 

Midway thru the Police chase, Trevor takes a left into the tunnel. You can either take the road on the right side or....

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "FOLLOW THE LEADER"

To achieve this requirement, follow Trevor into the tunnel. Weave your way thru traffic coming towards you and make it out on the other side.

PART 4: THE RACE BACK

 

When the chase ends, you can either take control of Michael, Trevor, or Franklin and then race back to Los Santos. Drive to the car garage to complete the mission.

MISSION NOTE: RACE TO THE FINISH

It is NOT necessary to win the race back to Los Santos after taking the cars.
