Tow Truck

100% Gold Requirements for Tow Truck
Mission Time Complete within 3:00
Not A Scratch Deliver the Tow Truck with no damage
Truckin' Reach top speed in the tow truck
 

Drive over to the tow truck location to see a lone truck next to a mechanic fixing his car. Sneak up behind the old man and smack him.

 

From there, grab the Tow truck and drive it back to the FIB lot (use the nearby shortcut on the hill to get on the freeway faster). The option of how you drive the truck to the FIB lot depends on the criteria below:

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "NOT A SCRATCH/ TRUCKIN"

Drive the tow truck at blazing speed for about 10-15 seconds to satisfy the "Truckin" requirement. This should also satisfy the time limit of the mission.

For the "Not A Scratch" requirement, you'll have to omit the "Truckin" option and drive the vehicle in a careful manner.
