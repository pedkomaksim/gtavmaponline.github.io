Minor Turbulence

100% Gold Requirements for Minor Turbulence
Accuracy Finish with a shooting accuracy of at least 80%
Four Wheel Flier Exit the cargo plane in the Mesa

PART 1: FLIGHT PLAN

 

On a first playthrough, the mission is triggered by Franklin when he goes to Michael's house. After the cutscene, drive over to the airfield and get into the crop duster and fly out towards the skyline.

Tail the cargo plane while flying low and staying under the radar. You have a very low ceiling for the first several minutes of the mission. Don’t go much higher than fifty feet off the ground.

 

As the Merryweather plane flies towards Mount Chiliad, the radar limit is lifted and Trevor can fly at normal height. Steer the plane upwards and move towards the Merryweather plane.

PART 2: STEALING THE CARGO PLANE

 

As you get close, the back hatch opens and guards start firing rockets. Fly into the open hatch to crash the plane inside.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "ACCURACY"

Stay in cover and equip a short-range weapon (for better accuracy) and gun down the guards that run towards you. Only move forward when the coast is cleared of vehicles!

 

Once the lower deck is cleared, climb the ladder to the cockpit and gun down the pilot to take the controls of the plane.

 

Once you take control of the plane, steer towards the airfield and fly on back. After several seconds, a pair of fighter jets fly in for some fun. Continue to maneuver the plane towards the airfield and the fighter jets will eventually shoot missiles and destroy the plane.

PART 3: CRASH AND BURN

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "FOUR WHEEL FLIER"

For this requirement, do NOT jump out of the plane by yourself! Get into the nearby Mesa vehicle and drive it out of the plane's hatch. Once you've driven off the plane, exit out of the car to start parachuting.

 

Maneuver the parachute towards Trevor's safehouse and land safely on the ground to complete the mission.
