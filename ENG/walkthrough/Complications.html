Complications

100% Gold Requirements for Complications
Mission Time Complete within 5:00
Can't Touch This Take no damage during the fight with Simeon
Dirt Nap Knock out the gardener with a stealth attack

PART 1: BREAKING AND ENTERING

 

When the mission begins, drive on over to Michael’s house. You can enter through the front gates by jumping over the entrance door or by jumping the fence at the tennis courts.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENT: "DIRT NAP"

To achieve this requirement, Franklin needs to assault the gardener using only stealth. The gardener is at the front of the house, so quietly sneak up behind him and knock him out for the requirement.


 

To enter the mansion, climb on top of the pickup truck, climb onto the roof, and then jump into the open window.

 

Switch into stealth mode, then make your way downstairs. Once you’re at the bottom of the stairs, sneak down the hall. The garage entrance is just under the stairwell to your left. As long as the folks in the kitchen keep their backs to you everything will be fine.

PART 2: THE DRIVE BACK

 

Sneak into the garage and jump in the car and drive back to the dealership.

 

Midway thru the trip, Michael pops in for a little surprise chat. Continue up to the dealership and follow Michael's demands to trigger the next section.

PART 3: THE FIGHT

 

Control will shift to Michael. Beating up Simeon won’t be much of a challenge. Michael’s special ability allows him to slow down time, but even without that advantage an aggressive series of kicks and punches will bring the crooked car salesman down. Defeat Simeon to complete the mission.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "MISSION TIME/ CAN'T TOUCH THIS"

In order to make the mission in under 5 minutes, you'll need to be quick when fighting Simeon. Get in as many punches as fast as you can while dodging and countering him (remember, you need to be unscathed during the fight).
