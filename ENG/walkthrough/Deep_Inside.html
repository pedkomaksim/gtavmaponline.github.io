Deep Inside

100% Gold Requirements for Deep Inside
Not a Scratch Deliver the JB 700 with minimal damage
Fastest Speed Reach top speed in the JB 700
Stealthy Recasting Knock out the actor with a silent attack
Premature Ejector Use the ejector seat within 10 seconds
Second Strike Run over the actor as you escape in JB 700

PART 1: INFILTRATION

 

After arriving at the studio lot, Franklin calls Molly and she gives the details for the mission. After the call, jump over the locked door into the studio. Approach the first trailer and get into cover.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "STEALTHY RECASTING"

Go into Stealth Mode and quickly sneak up behind the actor and knock him out to trigger a quick cutscene.

 

With new clothes in hand, quickly make your way to the movie set and jump into the car and hightail it outta the studio. If you're going for a gold medal, you'll need to get in the car and set off before the actress has a chance to bail and run away.

PART 2: THE ESCAPE

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "SECOND STRIKE"

Just as you exit the studio lot, you'll notice the knocked out actor wandering outside his trailer. Run him over with the car before leaving the lot for the Gold.

 

Avoid driving too fast while the actress is in the car. She tends to grab the wheel, making the car move erratically so avoid high speeds to prevent crashing.

 

About three security personnel will chase after the car and hit it from behind. When prompted, drop the spikes onto the road to pop the tires of the security guys. Drop as many as needed until the coast is clear.

PART 3: GETTING RID OF THE FAT

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "PREMATURE EJECTOR"

After eliminating the security cars, Franklin will ask, "Hey, what's this red button do?" and a prompt will instruct you to press the button to use the ejector seat. Press the button quickly to eject the actress out of the car.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "NOT A SCRATCH/ FASTEST SPEED"

The JB700 vehicle is a pretty fast vehicle, so as long as you can maintain full speed for about 15 seconds (after you've ejected the actress), this will be a Gold like no other.

As for "Not A Scratch", as long Frank delivers the JB700 with both headlights attached and the hood still intact, he'll get the Gold.

 

When the security cars are gone and the actress long gone, drive over to the waypoint at Devon's garage to complete the mission.
