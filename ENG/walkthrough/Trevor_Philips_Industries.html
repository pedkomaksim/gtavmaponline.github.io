Trevor Philips Industries

100% Gold Requirements for Trevor Philips Industries
Body Count Kill 32 enemies
Unmarked Complete with minimal damage to health and armor
Scrap Man Destroy 6 vehicles
Mission Time Complete within 4:30

PART 1: STORE SHOOT-OUT

 

When the mission begins, drive your associates to the lab and get ready for a shootout. Get into position and mow down the approaching Aztecas.

 

Eliminate the first couple of Aztecas that drive up until a pickup truck zooms past the building. From there, follow Chef outside and pick off the remaining Aztecas.

PART 2: ROOFTOP SHOOT-OUT

 

More Aztecas will appear from the other side of the building, so follow Chef and he'll pass you a sweet little Grenade Launcher.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "scrap man"

With the Grenade Launcher in hand, aim and blow up all the vehicles that zoom in on the parking lot below. Be sure to blow up SIX of them to complete the objective!

PART 3: FRONT DOOR SHOOT-OUT

 

After blowing up several more cars, Chef prompts you to follow him downstairs. Get into cover and shoot some more of the Aztecas until Chef gives the greenlight that the coast is clear. After the baddies are gone, approach the two men in the freezer to complete the mission.
