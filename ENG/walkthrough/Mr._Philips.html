Mr. Philips

100% Gold Requirements for Mr. Philips
No Survivors Kill all fleeing bikers
Headshots Kill 12 enemies with headshots
Mission Time Complete within 12 minutes
Trailer Trashed Cause $5000 damage to Ortega's Trailer
Lost and Damned Kill Terry and Clay during the chase


PART 1: CELEBRATION

 

The mission technically begins with either Michael or Franklin. Go to Michael's house and enter the living room to trigger a cutscene with Trevor.

MISSION NOTE: KILLING ASHLEY

In the PS3/X360 versions, Trevor has the option to shoot Ashley after the incident with Johnny.

In the PS4/X1 versions, the game will NOT allow Trevor to fire any weapons at her nor kill her.


PART 2: THE CHASE

MISSION NOTE: ARMOR AND AMMO STOCKUP!

After the opening cutscene, take the few minutes to stop by Ammu-Nation for some Body Armor and extra ammo.

Once you're stocked and primed, jump into Trevor's truck and drive over to the biker gang waypoint. From there, a short cutscene occurs followed by a chase!

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENT: "LOST AND DAMNED"

To achieve this requirement, shoot or run down Terry and Clay while following the black van. Use drive-by shooting to get them both.

On next-gen versions, killing Clay and Terry in First-Person mode is doable but the aiming reticle will be too far, so be cautious!

 

Get in the truck and follow the gang to their hideout. Midway thru the chase, you can go off-road and find a Stunt Jump just to the left of this bridge. It's not necessary to do the jump but you'll achieve your first USJ if you do.

PART 3: TRAILER PARK SHOOT-OUT

 

Once the gang arrives at the trailer park, take cover from the left side, while shooting whoever is closest. Trevor will only have a Shotgun equipped so you'll need to move from cover to cover to do some bad damage. There is a Health Pack just up the road near pickup truck, so grab it if needed and continue up the road and shooting and taking cover.

MISSION NOTE: RAGE ABILITY

Trevor’s special "Rage" ability allows him to slow time and reduce damage while causing double damage with his own shots. It isn't necessary to use it, but activate it if he gets too overwhelmed.
 

Your radar will occasionally reveal bikers zooming toward you in vehicles. Lay in wait and pick them off from cover as they pass by.

 

After several of the bikers are gunned down, the rest will retreat and run away from the area, leaving Trevor and his crew alone. From there, get in the truck and drive to the next waypoint.

PART 4: VISITING MR. ORTEGA

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "TRAILER TRASHED"

When you arrive at the destination, drag the trailer into the river to trigger a cutscene with Ortega.

From there, either shoot Ortega or walk away.

 

Once that's done, get back in your truck and drive home to complete the mission.
