The Construction Assassination

100% Gold Requirements for The Construction Assassination
Time Taken Complete in under 7:00
No Fly Zone Shoot down the targets helicopter
Money Earned Base Reward

The Construction Assassination is the final Assassination Mission in GTA 5. Before starting the mission, switch to all three characters and invest all your money in Gold Coast (GCD) on the LCN. Once that's done, begin the mission.

 

When you arrive at the construction site, there will be several guards watching all sides of the building. Stealth is not necessary but you can use a suppressed Sniper rifle to get thru them faster.

 

There are at least 6-7 guards surrounding the premises, so snipe them all down and then approach the elevator and take it to the next floor.

 

When you reach the next floor, quickly get into cover! Fight your way across the vacant floor towards the next elevator (watch out for the two shooters inside).

 

Take the elevator to the rooftop and take cover! Target the nearest baddies (including the lousy sniper on the crane above) and then shoot the helicopter down.

 

Once the chopper is down, eliminate the rest of the guards to clear the rooftop. To escape the area, take the nearby Parachute and jump off the northern section.

 

Make a safe landing on the ground to complete the mission. From this point, give the Gold Coast stock about 12-24 in-game hours to reach 80%. When it reaches that mark, sell all your assests for large profit!
