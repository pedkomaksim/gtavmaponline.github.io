Scouting the Port

100% Gold Requirements for Scouting The Port
Mission Time Complete within 20:00
Employee of the Month Complete without damaging the containers
Perfect Surveillance Snap all 3 photographs of the boat as instructed
An Honest Day's Work Complete without causing a disturbance at the docks

PART 1: THE DRIVE TO THE DOCKS

 

When the mission begins, get into Trevor's truck and drive over to the LS Docks. Park the vehicle near the dispatch office and follow Floyd to his boss and then follow him thru the docks.

PART 2: DIRTY DAY'S WORK

 

During the chat, a foreman will put Trevor and Floyd on a job moving containers from one section of the dock to another. Get into the large machine and drive towards the blue-colored containers that say "THRIFTEX".

Get in close and then lower the magnet and lift it up (press RIGHT on the D-Pad to make the container stick) and then drive it to the other side. Do it for both containers to complete this job.

 

Once that job is done, you’ll then move to the cargo crane. Climb the ladder and stairway and make it to the top of the crane where another foreman will ask for your help in moving containers from the back end to the front, where a awaiting truck is standing by.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "AN HONEST DAY'S WORK/ EMPLOYEE OF THE MONTH"

Lower the crane and pick up the container and then zoom down towards the truck on the other end and place the container on it's bed. When done correctly the crane will automatically reset itself. Repeat for the second container to complete this job.

As long as you don't cause a ruckus at the docks before snapping the photos for Ron, you should score these two objectives easily.

PART 3: CASING THE SHIP

When the second job is done, it's time to snap some photos! Go to the edge of the crane and whip out the cameraphone.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "PERFECT SURVEILLANCE"

PHOTO #1: Snap a photo of the FRONT of the ship.

PHOTO #2: Snap a photo of a guard on the ship.

PHOTO #3: Snap a wide photo of the back of the ship to complete this objective. With all three photos snapped, send them to Ron.

PART 4: STEALING THE MANIFEST

 

Climb back down from the crane and enter the truck below with Floyd and drive over to the docks on the south side.

 

Drive to the waypoint and exit the truck to trigger a cutscene. Once Trevor gets hold of the briefcase, quickly run out of the docks and find the nearest vehicle and drive back to Floyd's condo to complete the mission.

PLANNING THE MERRYWEATHER HEIST

 

If you choose Plan A, it will require only one additional mission. From there, it will require Franklin to use his sniping skills while Michael goes for Stealth.

If you choose Plan B, it will require TWO additional missions plus Trevor will request for Michael to take lessons at Flight School. While Flight School is not necessary for the mission, it will be helpful in getting the mission done quicker.
