Legal Trouble

100% Gold Requirements for Legal Trouble
Mission Time Complete within 5:30
Floor It Reach top speed in any car
Clean Escape Lose wanted level within 2:00
News Hound View Weasel News camera for 0:15 seconds

PART 1: THE STREET CHASE

 

After the cutscene, get into the closest fast vehicle and zoom over to Los Santos Airport to meet up with Molly. As you reach the airport, Molly drives onto the tarmac and causing a ton of chaos that Mike needs to evade.

PART 2: THE AIRPORT CHASE

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "FLOOR IT/ NEWSHOUND"

At any time during the chase, press the Circle/B button to view the news chopper camera. Be aware that switching to the news chopper will make Mike lose control of the car! Switch views every few seconds for the Gold. And when following Molly, gun it at full speed!

 

Continue to follow Molly thru the tarmac while carefully evading the explosions and flying Police cars until Molly reaches a hangar.

PART 3: THE FOOT CHASE

 

Once she flees her car, chase after her (there's no need to pull out a gun on her) until a gruesome scene occurs.

 

From there, pick up the film canister and get outta there! Just across from hangar exit is a private jet that Michael can steal. Enter the jet and quickly fly out of there!

PART 4: THE ESCAPE

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "MISSION TIME/ CLEAN ESCAPE"

Fly towards the ocean so that the Police lose sight of you (remember to out up the landing gears to ascend higher). Avoid flying in the city otherwise, you'll take longer to lose the Wanted level!
