Firetruck (Firetruck Access)

100% Gold Requirements for Fire Truck
What is your Emergency? Call 911 to contact the fire department
Not a Scratch Deliver the Fire Truck with no damage

For this mission, you are required to obtain a Firetruck vehicle. You can try to steal a truck from one of the Fire stations for an easy completion, but in order to get Gold, you'll need to use some creative tactics.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "WHAT IS YOUR EMERGENCY?"

To obtain the requirement, open up your cellphone and call the Emergency Services. Pick the Fire Department to have them sent to your current location.

 

Once the firetruck arrives, walk up to it and steal the truck. You'll get pegged with a 2-star Wanted level, so quickly stop and get out and find a good hiding spot (so that the cops don't try to ram you down).

 

Be aware that once the Wanted level disappears, you have to make sure all the cops are gone. If they see you roaming around, you WILL get pegged with another Wanted level, so be on the lookout!

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "NOT A SCRATCH"

When the coast is finally cleared, carefully drive the fire truck to the garment factory to complete the mission.
