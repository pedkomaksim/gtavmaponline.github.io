Mission Time
 Complete within 12:00
Not a Scratch
 Deliver the JB 700 with minimal damage
Shredder
 Disable 3 cop cars with spikes

MISSION NOTE: WHICH CHARACTER?

There are two ways to begin the mission:

  • If you start the mission as Trevor, the mission waypoint will be in front of Lamar's house in Strawberry. From there, Trevor will receive a call from Lamar and the game then switches to Franklin on his way to pick up Lamar.
  • If you start the mission as Franklin, Lamar will be waiting behind the garage shop.

PART 1: COUNTRYSIDE DRIVE

 

After picking up Lamar, drive over to Devon Westin's garage where a big rig is parked on the street. Drive the car into the big rig and then make the long drive to Paleto Bay.

 

Once you get near Paleto Bay, the police track you down in hot pursuit. From there, Franklin will exit the truck and get into the JB700.

PART 2: POLICE INTERFERENCE

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "SHREDDER/ NOT A SCRATCH"

Once the game switches to Franklin, immediately press the L3 button to start dropping spikes on the road. A quick cutscene will show the cop cars getting their tires blowed up. Keep tossing spikes on the ground and keep watch of the vehicle! You need both headlights and tail lights to be in good condition to get the "Not A Scratch" requirement!

 

After all the cop cars are eliminated, drive towards the waypoint at Procopio Beach to meet with Molly and complete the mission.
