Marriage Counseling

100% Gold Requirements for Marriage Counseling
Not a Scratch Complete with little damage done to the Bison
Mission Time Complete within 5:30
Drive-By Killer Kill 3 enemies while in a vehicle

PART 1: THE DRIVE

 

After the cutscene, get into the pickup truck and follow after the tennis instructor as he ascends up the hills in the red-colored car.

 

Midway during the chase, a hippy van will block the way. We can shoot a bullet or two to make van go away faster. You cannot avoid this van no matter what (unfortunately), so just drive around it and a waypoint will then appear on the GPS. 

PART 2: BRINGIN' DOWN THE HOUSE

 

Drive over to the waypoint to trigger a cutscene. From there, rev up the pickup truck to start leveling the house. It takes three tugs to bring the house tumbling down, so get to it!

PART 3: DRIVE-BY KILLER

 

After the house is totaled, drive back to Michael's house to trigger a phone call from the owner of the house, who then sends a few hitmen after you.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "DRIVE-BY KILLER/ NOT A SCRATCH"

After Michael answers the call from the house owner, she sends two cars after you with several hitmen. Rather than escape them, stop your car and do a Drive-by shooting on them when they get close. Remember, the car does NOT need to be in motion to do a Drive-by!

Rotate the camera and start spraying bullets on all of them to complete this requirement. Also, as long as the truck isn't totaled after the drive-by, you'll score the "Not A Scratch" medal.

 

When the coast is clear, drive back to Michael's house to complete the mission.

POST-MISSION CUTSCENE

 

After the mission, a quick cutscene shows Michael giving a call to Lester to setup the Jewelry Heist missions. Take note that on the PS4 version, Lester's conversation can only be heard on the Dualshock controller.
