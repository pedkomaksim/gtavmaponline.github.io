The Wrap Up

100% Gold Requirements for The Wrap Up
Mission Time
 Complete within 7:00
Headshots
 Kill 18 enemies with headshots
Buzz Off
 Take down the pursuing helicopter

PART 1: THE MEET-UP

 

When you arrive at Kortz Center, take the stairway at the very back of the courtyard next to the Bell Building entrance. Meet with Dave at the balcony to trigger the mission.

PART 2: THE SHOOT-OUT

 

Once everything goes down, take cover and stay in cover! All your enemies have tactical armor and semi-automatic rifles, so be smart.

 

Fight your way across the second floor while taking cover at every corner. Don't go guns blazing or you'll get shot down fairly quickly!

PART 3: HELP FROM TREVOR

 

Midway thru the balcony, a chopper flies in to ruin your party. From here, take control of Trevor and shoot down the chopper pilot quickly before he kills Michael.

 

After the chopper goes down, zoom in on the NOOSE agents on the ground floor and take them down one-by-one with sniper fire.

 

Switch back to Michael and fight your down the second floor towards the courtyard. Take cover at all times and watch out for another chopper!

 

Meet up with Dave by the reflective pool area and be ready to shoot down another chopper. If you've got the Rail Gun, it'll take two good shots to blow it up, so do it!

 

Switch to Trevor again and snipe down the two Merryweather guys on the balcony to clear the courtyard. From there, switch back to Michael and make your escape out of the Kortz.

PART 4: THE ESCAPE

 

Gun down the remaining Merryweather guys and get in the fast car nearby.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "BUZZ OFF"

As you leave the Kortz, one more chopper will fly in to shoot at you. Stop the car and shoot it down with Rockets or the Rail Gun for the Gold.

 

With the heat off your tail, drive to the Pawn shop in Morningwood and meet with Trevor to complete the mission.
