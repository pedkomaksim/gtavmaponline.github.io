Bugstars Equipment

100% Gold Requirements for Bugstars Equipment
Sneaky Pest Steal The Bugstars Van without being detected

Drive over to the LS docks to begin this mission. The Bugstars van is located inside a warehouse and guarded by three Bugstar employees.

If you enter from the front, the employees will detect you but if you enter the warehouse from the back....

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENT: "SNEAKY PEST"

To achieve this requirement, Mike has to steal the van without getting detected. Drive around to the back of the warehouse to see the Bugstar employee behind the van.

Use stealth to sneak up to him and then smack him. From there, get in the van and drive away from the BACK of the warehouse.


 

With the van in tow, drive it to the waypoint at the garment factory to complete the mission.
