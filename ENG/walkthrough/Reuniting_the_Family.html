Reuniting the Family

100% Gold Requirements for Reuniting the Family
Mission Time
 Complete within 10:30

PART 1: AMANDA & FABIAN

 

When the mission starts, get in the vehicle and drive to the waypoint at the Bean Machine. Get out of the car and walk up to Amanda to trigger a cutscene.

PART 2: TRACEY & LAZLOW

 

Once that's done, get back in the car and drive to the tattoo shop at Vinewood to meet with Tracey and Lazlow. When prompted, use all the options to give Lazlow a brand new look.

PART 3: DR, FRIEDLANDER

 

From there, get in the car and drive to Dr. Friedlander's office to trigger some family time.

PART 4: HOME SWEET HOME

Once that's done, drive back to Mike's house to complete the mission.
