100% Gold Requirements for Military Hardware
Head Hunter Kill 5 enemies with headshots
Sticky Strategist Stop the convoy using a sticky bomb
 

Stealing the military convoy requires some precise planning in order to pull it off smoothly. First, get within a very far distance of the moving convoy and park your vehicle vertically so that it blocks the way.

 

Next, place some Sticky Bombs on the road to begin the set up. From there, get behind cover near your vehicle and wait for the convoy to drive towards you.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "STICKY STRATEGIST"
