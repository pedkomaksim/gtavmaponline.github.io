Friends Reunited

100% Gold Requirements for Friends Reunited
Headshots Kill 5 enemies with headshots
Mystery Gift Destroy the trailers without being detected
Unmarked Complete with minimal damage to health and armor
Perfect Gift Destroy all trailers at once

PART 1: A LITTLE GOING AWAY GIFT

 

When the mission starts, drive to the waypoint at Lost MC the trailer park. Once there, you have several objectives to complete.

 

The trailer park attack is a matter of patience. Go around the perimeter while avoiding the bikers for the moment and attach the Sticky Bombs to each trailer. The Sticky Bombs can also be thrown at a high arc, so rather than approaching the trailers in the center of the area, toss the bombs at them to avoid detection (don't worry, Trevor's got a good throwing arm).

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENT: "HEADSHOTS"

To achieve the "Headshots" requirement, Trevor can sneak up on a few of the lone bikers and snipe them. There are a total of SEVEN bikers in the surrounding area, two of which are in duos, so snipe them quickly before they raise the alert.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENT: "MYSTERY GIFT/ PERFECT GIFT"

If you are aiming for these two requirement, Trevor must NOT get detected while placing all five bombs. Place all five Sticky Bombs in place and then detonate them to gain this achievement.

PART 2: BEACHSIDE ROAD TRIP

 

With the trailer park is destroyed, get back in the truck and drive to Los Santos. The next waypoint will be just above Vinewood Hills where Trevor makes a short pitstop. From there, drive down to Vespucci Beach to complete the mission.
