Crystal Maze

100% Gold Requirements for Crystal Maze
Headshots Kill 10 enemies with headshots
Unmarked Complete with minimal damage to health and armor
2 Birds, 1 Stone Kill 2 enemies with one shot
Accuracy Finish with an accuracy of at least 80%

PART 1: FRIENDLY RIVALRY

 

Drive to the O’Neil farm. From this vantage point, select your suppressed Sniper Rifle. If you don't have a Suppressor for the Sniper Rifle, buy one! This will allow Trevor to get in several silent headshots on the hillbillies before he gets detected.

GOLD MEDAL REQUIREMENTS: "HEADSHOTS/ 2 BIRDS, 1 STONE"

For the "2 Birds, 1 Stone" achievement, Trevor needs to equip the Heavy Rifle, which is a more powerful sniper rifle that can easily kill two men in one shot. Approach the two brothers shooting at cans near the barn and snipe them from behind for a 2-for-1 kill.

[Be careful when using the Heavy Rifle because it is NOT available with a Suppressor meaning any shots you fire will be heard!]

 

As soon as you snipe the two fellows plinking at cans, pan above and clear the balcony. Work down and right from there. If you accidentally alert the farm, kill as many defenders as you can with your rifle and then switch to your shotgun and assault the farm.

PART 2: INDOOR SHOOT-OUT

 

Once the defenders are cleared outside, enter the farmhouse from the left side. Use corners for cover, and fire as you turn corners. Use Trevor’s special ability to clear the halls.

 

The door to the basement is in the far side of the lower floor of the house. Make your way down and shoot this guy before he pulls a gun.

PART 3: BLOWIN' UP THE HOUSE

 

Grab a Jerry Can and make a trail of gas from the basement through the house and outside.

 

Ignite the trail with a gunshot and then hop on the ATV and hightail it outta there to complete the mission.
