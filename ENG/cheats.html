Cheat Codes

How to Use Cheat Codes

GTA V, like the games that have come before it, has a variety of cheat codes that can be used to help wreak havoc upon the city of Los Santos. There are three primary ways to enter cheats:

  • (Keyboard)
    Press the tilde (~) key in the upper left corner or the keyboard. Then type the command. (Not case sensitive)
  • (Phone)
    Bring up your phone by pressing the Up arrow, go to contacts, and then press the middle mouse button to bring up the numpad and enter the cheat number. Press the spacebar to dial.
  • (Gamepad)
    If you are using an Xbox or PlayStation controller, the same button combinations for the Xbox One or PlayStation 4 can be used.

NOTE: Once a cheat code is activated, achievements will be disabled for that session.
