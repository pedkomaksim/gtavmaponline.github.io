Special Actor Locations | Humans of Los Santos Achievement There are 12 Special Actors to use in Director Mode each of which you have to find throughout Los Santos. Upon meeting them, their direct mode availability can be unlocked by talking to them. Unlocking all 12 will unlock the Humans of Los Santos achievement. The players are listed alphabetically below.
Animal Locations | Cryptozoologist achievement There are 27 plant locations which provides hallucination effects turning you in a different animal depending which peyote plant you visit. Upon finding the final hallucination and entering it, you should unlock this achievement. You do not have to follow this order, but it'll provide an effective route to take in visiting all peyote plants.
GTA Online: All About Daily Objectives Daily Objectives are three random tasks given to each players who has rank 15 or higher each day that will net cash and RP awards when completed.
How to Become a Billionaire in Story Mode Warning: This guide may contain slight spoilers, however this is kept to a very minimum.
This guide will show you a ROUGH tutorial on how to become a 'billionaire' on Grand Theft Auto V Story Mode. Please read EVERYTHING in order to get it all right. By the end of this guide, you are guaranteed to acquire over $300,000,000 for each individual character. I did end up with only $800,000,000, although that is because the stock market was not in my favour at the time. Just to remind you, it doesn't matter if you only get $300,000,000 because three quaters of it will never be spent anyway. Hope this helps you out a lot!
