Questions and Answers
I've come across some questions that have either been asked frequently or that I feel should be shown hear with an answer. So I made a Q&A section for this guide to maybe help those of you who haven't visited yet.

I've completed all of the Assassination missions for Story Mode. Is there another way I can earn money?
The answer is no. Your best bet is an old save or if not, restarting Story Mode is the only option. The Stock Markets are incredibly unpredictable and if you want to try to get lucky on them without the Assassination missions, you're going to still be there on the release of Grand Theft Auto 6.

I'm losing profit on my investement! Am I doing something wrong?
Yes or no. If you've saved when you have started seeing a decrease, yes. Otherwise no. You can just reload your save and sell your market shares when it's at its highest.

I did the first assassination (Assassination 1: The Hotel Assassination) before I did the other missions. Should I do the other assassination missions before I continue with the story missions?
No. As I said, the first assassination isn't really crucial and it has to be completed before the end of Story Mode. However the rest of the Assassination Missions must be left until the end of Story Mode.

The Tinkle Random Event isn't appearing! What should I do?
This has been brought up a couple of times before. Be patient and give it time. I was able to make him appear straight away from heading towards Los Santos on the highway on a Monday afternoon. There is no reason to why he shouldn't spawn unless you have already accidently done that mission.

Is there anyway to not sit there for hours clicking the + button to invest?
This may seem dumb to some people but it's a common mistake. You can make it rise faster by simply holding down left click on the + button.

Why should you start the those Assassination Missions on a Monday or Tuesday?
On Monday's or Tuesday's it shoots up the highest and the quickest.
